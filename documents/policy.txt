FinanceCore Bank Policy Manual

Refund Policy:
All refund requests must be submitted within 30 days of transaction.
Refunds above Rs 50,000 require manager approval.
Fraudulent transactions are refunded within 24 hours.

Loan Policy:
Personal loans require minimum credit score of 700.
Business loans require 2 years of financial statements.
Interest rates range from 8.5% to 14% annually.

Compliance Policy:
All transactions above Rs 10 lakh must be reported to RBI.
KYC verification is mandatory for all new accounts.
Suspicious transactions must be flagged within 2 hours.

Employee Policy:
Employees cannot access customer data without authorization.
All data access is logged and audited quarterly.
Violations result in immediate suspension pending investigation.
